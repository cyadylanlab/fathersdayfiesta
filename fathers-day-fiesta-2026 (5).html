Welcome to the Fiesta

Virginia Valor FC, in partnership with SYA and CYA Soccer, invites your club to the Father’s Day Fiesta All-Star Tournament. This event is built for recreational and rec all-star teams from across Northern Virginia and beyond.

This is not a travel showcase or an ID camp. It is for the teams that show up week after week because they enjoy playing together. The families who are on the sidelines every weekend. The volunteer coaches who give their time to make it all happen.

Over the course of two days, teams will play competitive, well-organized games in a positive and welcoming environment. With Father’s Day as the backdrop, it is a weekend that brings together good soccer and time with family.

Whether your team has been together for years or just finished their first season, the Fiesta is a great way to close out the spring. It is a chance to compete, represent your club, and create some lasting memories along the way.

What to Expect

We have been running youth soccer in this community for a long time, and we have put together every detail of this tournament with that experience behind it. Here is what your team and families can look forward to:

Competitive, fair brackets. We group teams thoughtfully so every game is a good one. No blowouts, no walkovers — real competition at every level from U8 through U19.

Well-maintained fields. All games are played across SYA and CYA’s home complexes in Centreville and Chantilly — fields our clubs know and maintain year-round.

Professionally managed. Licensed referees, clear schedules, on-site tournament HQ at every location, and a staff that has been doing this for years. Your job is to coach and cheer. We will handle everything else.

A real weekend event. This is more than a few games stapled together. It is a full tournament weekend with awards, team photos, and the kind of atmosphere that makes your players feel like they are part of something special.

Tournament Details

Who Can Play? +
The Father’s Day Fiesta is open to recreational and rec all-star teams only. This is a tournament built for the recreational soccer community — no travel or club teams. Boys and girls divisions are available at every age group from U8 through U19.
Roster Entry — Deadline June 7 +
Log in to your PlayMetrics account and follow these steps:
  • Go to “My Teams” (do not click the purple “Register” button — your team is already registered)
  • Click your team name, then “Manage Roster”
  • Enter each player’s name, date of birth, and jersey number
  • Add contact info for coaches, assistant coaches, and team managers
  • Use the “More Actions” tab to edit jersey numbers after entry
Game-Day Check-In — Saturday, June 20 +
Before your first game, stop by your game site’s tournament HQ to check in. You will need to bring:
  • Official team roster signed by your club administrator. You can use our roster form or your own club’s version — just make sure it has a verifying club signature. Every player needs a unique jersey number (tape or marker is fine).
  • Team check-in form — coaches will certify on this form that they have completed, signed medical release forms in their possession for every rostered player and will carry them at every game.
Medical Release Policy +
New this year: We are not collecting medical release forms at check-in. Instead, coaches will certify on the team check-in form that they have a signed medical release for every rostered player and will have those forms on hand at every game. Our tournament form, your club’s form, or any standard medical release form will work.
Fields & Locations +
Games will be played across SYA and CYA field complexes in Centreville and Chantilly. Detailed field maps, parking information, and site-specific tournament HQ locations will be sent to all registered teams before the tournament. View general field info →
